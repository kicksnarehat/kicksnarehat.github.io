<p>Three early shows this Sunday:</p>
<ol>
<li>John Powhida and JPX-"ish" (w/ special guests) play <a href="https://www.songkick.com/concerts/27431484" target="_blank">Making Waves for Autism</a> at Sally O'Brien's. This is a benefit concert for <a href="http://www.surfershealing.org/" target="_blank">Surfer's Healing</a> -- a truly amazing free surf camp for children with autism. My son has attended before, and will be going again this year. Ask me about it at the show and I'll try to tell you the story without crying. The Dogmatics and Girl On Top also perform.  JPX plays @1:30 sharp! Hawaiian Shaved Ice Stand and prizes.</li>
<li>Hillbilly Holiday plays <a href="https://www.songkick.com/concerts/27431489" target="_blank">Aeronaut Brewing at 2:30pm</a> for the "Media Mixer" event. No cover charge, great venue, great beer, great hang!</li>
<li>The Weisstronauts play Aeronaut Brewing at 3:30pm (finally, I get a moment's rest between sets). No cover, great venue... all that stuff plus <strong>more guitars</strong> (and less/no singing).</li>
</ol>
