I'm playing a string of shows with NYC artist Jack Grace this week -- including one here in Cambridge on Friday, 12/18 at Toad. Jack is a serious entertainer and talented songwriter who's collaborated with Norah Jones and penned songs for feature films... and he's weird, people. Good weird. He doesn't come to the Boston area much, so if you're on the fence, hop on over.

We're also playing Norey's in Newport, RI on Wednesday, 12/16 and Strange Brew Tavern in Manchester, NH on 12/19.
