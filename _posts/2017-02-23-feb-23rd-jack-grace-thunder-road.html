Tonight will be my first gig at Thunder Road, the little club that could (previously, couldn't) in Union Square, Somerville. It's bigger and cooler now, and to prove it Jack Grace is opening the evening with Hillbilly Holiday/Baby Ray guitarist Ken Lafler on bass. Not to be out-done, guitarist Pete Weiss will be performing with every band in New England (soon), starting tonight with aforementioned Brooklyn baddie* Jack Grace and then with trouble-troubadour Haley Thompson-King. The Mallet Brothers headline. Doors are 7pm, and we're on first, so... 7:30 or 8?
