Reads like we're touring in a mini, doesn't it? Actually it's just a short tour down to NC and back, starting with a free event at Armory Sound. Here's the entire run-down:

Tuesday May 13, 2014: Armory Sound, formerly Hi-N-Dry, Somerville MA

A special, intimate "tour kickoff" on our home turf.  Early show, 7pm.  This is a special edition of Armory Sound Live Series --  we'll be professionally recording this set for possible release.

Wednesday May 14, 2014: 68 Jay Street Bar, Brooklyn NY

We're excited to play this very cool new-ish venue with our pal Jack Grace and his cast of musical cohorts!

Thursday May 15, 2014, Rodeo Bar, New York NY

Always happy to return to the Rodeo Bar.  We'll be playing two sets of instrumental twang, surf, rock, and psychedelia.  No cover at the Rodeo!

Friday May 16, 2014, Treehouse Lounge, Washington DC

Early set!  We start the evening off at 7pm and are followed by our pals the Jelly Roll Mortals and then Gramophonic, the Federal Hillbillies and See Rule Five.

Satuday May 17, 2014: MotorCo, Durham NC

A 4pm matinee appearance, as part of the annual INSTRO SUMMIT -- the world's biggest instrumental rock festival!

Monday May 19, 2014: Armory Sound, formerly Hi-N-Dry, Somerville MA

Yes, we are "bookending" this tour!  A special, intimate "tour wrap-up" on our home turf.  Early show, 7pm.  This is a special edition of Armory Sound Live Series --  we'll be professionally recording this set for possible release.

Friday May 23, 2014, Pleasant Valley Brewing, Saxtons River VT

Two sets at our fave Vermont venue!  Great food and drink here... Music goes from 8pm-10:30pm, no cover.
