We're thrilled to be on such a great bill Saturday at the Lizard Lounge, sandwiched between Amy Correia and Fireking. Amy sings duet and backup vocals on our most recent recording (unreleased as of this posting), so it shouldn't be too difficult to get her back onstage for our set. Fireking's front-man Anthony Kaczynski and drummer Smitt E. Smitty played in Figures on a Beach a long, long time ago -- before you and I were born -- but strangely they have not aged at all. (If anyone has a copy of their eponymous Sire release stashed away in the attic, you may want to check to see if their photos have greyed and wrinkled.)
