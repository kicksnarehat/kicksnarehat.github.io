Another double-duty evening (luckily at the same club), and an usual pairing too: The Vivs play our first night at Sally O'Brien's in Union Square, with Hillbilly Holiday opening. Sure, there's a "female singers" theme if you're looking for continuity, but musically these bands are quite different -- which is why this is a great bill. Remember when clubs used to stretch a bit more putting acts together? Ah, the good old days...

HH opens at 9:30, and The Vivs follow around 11. Details on SongKick.
