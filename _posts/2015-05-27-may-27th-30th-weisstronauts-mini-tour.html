The Weisstronauts kick off a mini-tour tonight, May 27th,  with an early show at Cuisine en Locale in Somerville (7:30pm). It's a foodie bar (hence the name), and the food is very good. We'll be literally playing for our supper.

Here's the rest of the schedule:

Thursday May 28, 2015
Pleasant Valley Brewing
Saxtons River VT
8pm till 10:30pm.  No cover, great food and tap selection.

Friday May 29, 2015
B Side Ballroom & Supper Club
Oneonta NY
This will be our first time playing the B Side, though Pete & Nate played last year, as part of Jack Grace's band and LOVED the venue. Ridiculously good food, drink, vibe, sound... They care deeply about music here.

Saturday May 30, 2015
Spring Street Gallery
Saratoga Springs NY
With Greg DeLuis opening at 8pm. Weisstro-time: 9pm-11pm.

NOTE: the 'Nauts will continue the mini-tour with a second leg June 3rd-7th, though I'm not playing those dates. You can find out more details at the Weissy site.
