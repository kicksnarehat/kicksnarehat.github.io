Three very different shows this week, each paired with equally-different food themes:

July 24: I'm subbing with the Noteworthy Jazz Ensemble at Stellina's, an excellent Italian restaurant in Watertown Square. 6-8:30pm.

July 26: Hillbilly Holiday plays at the Wollaston Yacht Club from 8-10pm. Unlike the last show I played there (with the Weisstronauts), this one's open to the public. Nothing fancy, just a friendly (and kid-friendly) hang on Wollaston beach in Quincy with a spectacular evening view on the covered deck overlooking the water and the Boston skyline. $5 cover and lots of free parking right along the beach. Kenny recommends Tony's Clam Shop, just down the street. Details on Songkick. [Check out Hillbilly Holiday's new EP on BandCamp!]

July 27: JPX plays at the Plough and Stars. Music starts at 10:30, but the food -- not sure what to call it, maybe just amazing? -- starts earlier... Details on Songkick.
