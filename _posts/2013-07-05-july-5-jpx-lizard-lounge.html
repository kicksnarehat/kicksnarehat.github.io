JPX plays with A.K.A.C.O.D. tonight at the Lizard Lounge. Tom Janovitz opens (solo), then 2 sets by A.K.A.C.O.D., and JPX closes. We have a special guest tonight -- for one show only -- Jim Gambino (Swinging Steaks) will join us on keys. If you want to catch Monique & crew, go early because it may sell out. Then again, don't be discouraged if it does -- it's likely that many from the Hi-N-Dry fanclub may skip out after their set. If you get turned away early, hang out close by and help us clean up the place later!
