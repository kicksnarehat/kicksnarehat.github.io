The first of two 'Nauts Holiday Jubilees this year* is at the Plough & Stars, with music starting at 10. (Of course, dinner is recommended if you like eating excellent food prepared by other people.) Tradition dictates these annual events occur in a new venue with new guest bands each year, along with a heavy helping of holiday-themed (possibly even sung) numbers. Our Holiday Stenographer was asleep at the keyboard when this first gig was booked, though,  so we'll be hogging the stage for this not-so-silent-night. A few songs will get the mangled-lyric treatment, but the rumor mill is already churning and bubbling and doing whatever rumor mills do when they find out who might jump up on stage and grab the mic.  Very limited details of which are available on Songkick.

* this year's second Jubilee is 12/21 at the Midway Cafe; sadly I won't be there!
