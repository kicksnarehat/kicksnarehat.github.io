A stripped-down/mini-lineup of John Powhida International Airport will play upstairs at Once Ballroom this Saturday for the Rock & Roll Rumble 2017 as part of the "Winner Dinner" series, where past crown-holders open the night upstairs at the Once Lounge before the main event. Originally slated as a guitar/drum duo, we're now to be joined by the honorable Peter Moore on keys (+vox & "left-hand bass"). Kitchen opens at 6pm, JPX plays 7:30-8pm, and R&RR Preliminary Night #6 kicks off at 9pm.  More info at the Once website.
