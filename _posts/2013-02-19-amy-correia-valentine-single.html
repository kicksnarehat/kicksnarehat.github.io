A little bird told me this one deserves its own post: I'm a big fan of Amy Correia, so it was a special treat to play on her recent single, "Hot Dream".  I hadn't heard her before she joined JPX at Q-Division to sing on a few numbers -- one of which was released as a Christmas single (with more to be released in a few months) -- and I was floored by her live show and then by her albums. I highly recommend picking up her latest, "You Go Your Way" (and if you're reading this on Feb 19th, she's playing tonight at the Lizard Lounge).
