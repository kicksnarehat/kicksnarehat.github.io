Thursday is double-duty night! First up, an unusual event at Goorin Bros., the hat shop on Newbury Street, with an early show by the Bertrand Laurence Quartet (aka "Menage A Quatre") playing musettes and blues. Finger-style acoustic guitar, accordion, clarinet & brushwork starts shortly after 5 and ends around 9pm, with breaks for arm-wrestling somewhere in-between. FREE.

Later the same evening...  (say, eleven?) Hillbilly Holiday hits the stage in Somerville at Radio. The Darlings and the Phil Aiken Army play earlier if you're the stay-in-one-place type.
