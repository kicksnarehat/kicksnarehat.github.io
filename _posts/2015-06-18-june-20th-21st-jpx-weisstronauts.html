Three free (!) shows this weekend, beginning Saturday 6/20 afternoon when John Powhida International Airport plays the Arlington Alive Summer Arts Block Party. Fun in the sun starts at 4pm. Later that evening The Weisstronauts play Aeronaut Brewing Company in Somerville, around 9:30pm. (I seem to remember a good Taco Truck parked in the lot for events there.)

On Sunday 6/21, The 'Nauts play at The Les Paul Big Sound Experience music festival. Weisstro-time is 4:30 sharp on the Les Paul Stage. Note set times are only 30 minutes, so if you don't like what you hear, rest easy 'cuz it'll be over soon. In addition to the 9 bands playing, there's a mobile Les Paul museum exhibit.
