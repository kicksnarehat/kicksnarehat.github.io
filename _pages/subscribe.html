Read about upcoming shows right here. Easy right? The trick is knowing in advance when I'll post something... how about I call you (on your rotary-phone land line) and give you a heads-up? Well, that ain't gonna happen, so maybe you want to subscribe to an RSS feed, or try one of the methods below.

Twitter

Subscribe to kicksnarehat2 for notifications for all gigs/bands. This channel is for gigs only -- no "I just at __ for lunch" tweets, I promise.

SongKick

View calendars individually by band on SongKick (when viewing, click "track" to subscribe):

  • Hillbilly Holiday
  • John Powhida International Airport
  • The Vivs
  • The Weisstronauts

Caveat: I'll post an event on SongKick for each show that I'm playing, but SongKick listings don't imply my participation (JPX and the 'Nauts use sub drummers on occasion). Most drummers look the same under in low lighting situations, though, so you may not notice.

Old-School Email Notification

Email whenever I'm playing. Not a listserv, but a manually-maintained list (sent BCC to participants). Being lazy, the message might be a link to a recent posting here, or a SongKick event... who knows? Given the above options, I'll assume any unsubscribe requests are being superceded by the newer technology noted above, so no hard feelings!
